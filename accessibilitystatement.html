Accessibility Statement

Committed to Digital Inclusion

Last Updated: February 10, 2026

LiveGigs Asia is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

Conformance Status: Partially Conformant

1. Our Commitment

LiveGigs Asia believes in creating an inclusive digital environment where all users, regardless of ability, can access and enjoy our content. We strive to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards.

Our commitment includes regular accessibility audits, user testing with assistive technologies, and ongoing staff training on accessibility best practices.

2. Accessibility Standards

We aim to conform to the following standards and guidelines:

  • Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA
  • Accessible Rich Internet Applications (ARIA) 1.1
  • Hong Kong Digital Accessibility Standards
  • Section 508 of the US Rehabilitation Act (where applicable)

3. Accessibility Features

Our website includes the following accessibility features designed to enhance usability for all visitors:

Current Features

  • Keyboard navigation support for all interactive elements
  • Alt text for all meaningful images and graphics
  • Semantic HTML structure for screen reader compatibility
  • Sufficient color contrast ratios (minimum 4.5:1 for normal text)
  • Resizable text up to 200% without loss of functionality
  • Consistent navigation and identification across all pages
  • Form labels and error identification for all input fields
  • Skip links for bypassing repetitive content

4. Known Limitations

Despite our best efforts to ensure accessibility of LiveGigs Asia, there may be some limitations. Below is a description of known limitations and potential solutions:

  • Some third-party embedded content (videos, social media feeds) may not fully meet accessibility standards
  • Legacy PDF documents may not be fully accessible; we are working to update these
  • Some decorative animations may not respect reduced motion preferences

We are actively working to address these limitations and appreciate your patience as we continue to improve.

5. Compatible Assistive Technologies

Our website is designed to be compatible with the following assistive technologies:

  • Screen readers (NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver, TalkBack)
  • Screen magnification software
  • Speech recognition software
  • Keyboard-only navigation
  • Switch access devices
