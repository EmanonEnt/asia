Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: February 10, 2026

LiveGigs Asia is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

1. Our Commitment

LiveGigs Asia believes in creating an inclusive digital environment where all users, regardless of ability, can access and enjoy our content. We strive to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards.

2. Accessibility Standards

We aim to conform to the following standards and guidelines:

  • Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA
  • Accessible Rich Internet Applications (ARIA) 1.1
  • Hong Kong Digital Accessibility Standards

3. Accessibility Features

Our website includes the following accessibility features:

  • Keyboard navigation support for all interactive elements
  • Alt text for all meaningful images and graphics
  • Semantic HTML structure for screen reader compatibility
  • Sufficient color contrast ratios (minimum 4.5:1 for normal text)
